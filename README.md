# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Excel dashboard** designed to analyze and monitor e-commerce sales performance.
The dashboard provides a clear view of key business metrics such as **Revenue, Profit, Orders, Profit Margin, and Average Order Value**, allowing users to explore sales trends and performance across **time, product categories, and regions**.

The dashboard was built using **Excel PivotTables, PivotCharts, slicers, and dynamic KPI cards** to provide an intuitive and data-driven view of business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Track overall **sales and profitability**
* Analyze **sales trends over time**
* Identify **top performing regions and product categories**
* Monitor **key business KPIs**
* Provide actionable insights for **business decision-making**

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used

* Microsoft Excel
* PivotTables
* PivotCharts
* Slicers and Timeline Filters
* GETPIVOTDATA
* KPI Cards
* Data Visualization & Dashboard Design

---

## 📌 Key Features

* **Interactive KPI Cards**

  * Revenue
  * Total Profit
  * Total Orders
  * Profit Margin
  * Average Order Value

* **Dynamic Filtering**

  * Year filter
  * Product category filter
  * Region filter
  * Timeline for month-wise analysis

* **Visual Analytics**

  * Sales trend over time
  * Sales by region
  * Sales by category
  * Profit by category
  * Top 10 products by sales

## 📈 Key Insights

* The **Technology category generates the highest sales and profit** among all categories.
* The **West region contributes the largest share of total revenue**.
* Sales show **noticeable spikes during peak seasonal months**.
* A small group of **top-performing products drives a large portion of revenue**.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing and promotions on **high-performing categories such as Technology**.
* Expand operations or campaigns in **high-performing regions like the West**.
* Plan **inventory and marketing campaigns ahead of seasonal sales spikes** to maximize revenue.
